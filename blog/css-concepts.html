4 April 2025

Explanation of Contents, Margin, Border, and Padding

Here are the breif explanation of each different elements.

  • Content - The content of the box, text and images can appear here
  • Padding - An area covers around the content. It is a space between the content and the border
  • Border - A border that goes around the padding and content
  • Margin - An area covers outside the border. It is a space bewteen the border and external elements
