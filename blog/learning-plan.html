Learning Plan

27 April 2025

What is your long term goal and/or career pathway?

I would like to get involved in tech industry. After completing this course, I plan to work on personal projects where I can apply the skills I've learnt at Dev-Academy, continue improving, and grow into a better programmer.

What do you think your biggest strengths and limitations will be in Bootcamp?

I believe my biggest limitation will be my growth mindset. I tend to judge myself very strictly, which can affect my self-confidence and slow down my learning. However, I believe that by working on developing a stronger growth mindset, I can turn this into a future strength by acknowledging my weakness and using them as opportunities to improve.

What do you think your biggest non-technical challenge at Bootcamp will be?

My biggest non-technical challenge will be expressing my opinions or feedback openly. I sometimes hold back from being completely honset because I worry about how others might feel. During Bootcamp, I would like to practice sharing my thoughts and emotions more clearly and respectfully.

What non-technical skills - human skills - would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

I would like to improve my collaboration and communication skills so I can work more productively and efficiently with others. I also aim to strengthen my growth mindset to support both myself and my team members in staying resilient and motivated through challenges.

What are your expectations from the Bootcamp team?

I hope to receive helpful feedback on the projects that I and my future teams complete, from both our peers and facilitators. Constructive feedback will help me continue improving both technically and personally.

What are your expectations of yourself on Bootcamp

I expect myself to develop both my technical and human skills so that I can confidently move forward with my personal projects after Dev-Academy. I will fully participate in classes, ask for help when needed, and also offer support to others whenever I can.
